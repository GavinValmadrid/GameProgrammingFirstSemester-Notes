-Your game shouldn't be unpredictable.
-If you're making a side scroller game, your game should have an indicator of the hazards.
-Complex gameplay created by game rules
-Choices made by players
-Chance is the easiest to implement but not always the best way to implement unpredictability.
-Complex rules can be very difficult to design and make games overwhelming.
-Mechanic is more detailed and concrete than a rule
